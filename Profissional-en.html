Professional

Wind and Solar Resource Analysis

My area of expertise is wind and solar resource analysis, including data filtering and manipulation, resource extrapolation, long term correlation, simulation of wind and solar projects, micrositing and layout of wind turbines and solar trackers, P50, P75 and P90 calculations.

I have simulated approximately 20GW over diferent states in Brazil, with many different types of wind turbine, solar pannels and projects from the leading companies of the Brazilian energy sector. I have also analysed the data from over 200 met masts and solar stations.

Business and Project Development

I worked in the development of wind and solar projects, since the initial conception of the measurements campaign, basic project design, regulatory approvals in different project stages, registration for energy auctions, liasing with regulatory agencies (ANEEL, EPE, etc).

With experience in diferent stages of projects, I have a global vision of wind and solar projects and knowledge on how to take a project from zero into operation. Additionally, I analysed the technical aspects of many Merge and Acquire (M&A) opportunities and strategic partnerships.

Programming and Customized Solutions
